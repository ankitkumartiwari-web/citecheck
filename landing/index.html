Workflow

From PDF to verified answer in one focused loop.

01

Ingest

PDFs are parsed, chunked, embedded locally, and stored in ChromaDB.

02

Retrieve

The app pulls the most relevant passages for the question or claim.

03

Answer

The model writes from retrieved context and cites the passages it used.

04

Verify

A second pass labels claims as supported, partial, or unsupported.

Capabilities

Built like a real research tool, not a PDF chatbot demo.

Grounded Chat

Ask natural-language questions and inspect every cited source passage.

Claim Verification

A second pass labels each claim supported, partial, or unsupported against the text.

Cross-paper Compare

Check whether selected papers support, refute, or stay neutral on a claim.

Peer Review Panel

Three specialist reviewer agents plus a chair that synthesizes a decision.

Translate

Convert an indexed paper's text into another language in one pass.

Trained ML Tagging

A scikit-learn model tags each passage by rhetorical role, a real trained model.

Engineering

FastAPI, ChromaDB, local embeddings, a trained scikit-learn model, and OpenRouter-compatible LLMs.
